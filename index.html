This learning portal is going to be used to propose one of our research titles.


Our research is about Exploring the use of online collaborative tools in enhancing the teamwork skills of ICT students.


This learning portal is just going to be a prototype to propose our research on and will not be a fully functional application.





The research title that we want to propose is "Exploring the use of online collaborative tools in enhancing the teamwork skills of ICT students".



our Rationale"The researcher conducted this study because we wanted to understand the role of technology in modern education and to improve our teamwork skills on projects, share ideas, and to improved better communications, problem-solving,and overall collaboration abilities".

our Research Design "This research paper utilizes a Qualitative research design to gather data that are needed to this study, based on Fossey, Harvey, and McDermott (2002),Qualitative research aims to address questions concerned with developing an understanding of the meaning and experience dimensions of humansâ€™ lives and social worlds.This paper utilizes a Interviews with a different set of questions with the same difficulty. As the Qualitative research we aim to explore the use of online collaborative tools in enhancing the teamwork skills of ICT students."


our Respondents/Participants "The target participants of this study will be the Grade 11 Information Communication Technology (ICT) strand students at San Guillermo National High School (SGNHS)."


Data gathering tools and techniques "This study will be semi- structured interview. This approach allows for in-depth exploration of ICT students' and educators' experiences and perceptions regarding the use of these tools.Through open-ended questions, interviews will capture detailed insights into how online collaborative tools influence teamwork skills, providing a nuanced understanding of their impact in educational settings. The flexibility of semi-structured interviews also enables the researcher to delve deeper into specific areas of interest based on participants' responses."


our Statistical Tools or Method of Analysis "The interview data will be analyzed using theme analysis. Using this technique, important parts of the transcripts are methodically found and coded.These pieces are then categorized into more general themes. These themes will highlight important trends about how cooperation abilities are impacted by online collaboration tools. The analysis will offer a thorough grasp of the influence of these tools by examining, defining, and improving upon these themes. This understanding will be reported together with corroborating statements from the interviews."


our Ethical Consideration "By carefully considering and addressing these ethical considerations, The researchers can conduct their study responsibly and ethically, ensuring the well-being and rights of all participants while contributing to a better understanding of the use of online collaborative tools in education."
